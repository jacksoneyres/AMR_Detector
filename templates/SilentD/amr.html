Antimicrobial Resistance

Usage and Additional Info

Antimicrobial Resistance Detector determines the presence of resistance marker genes in bacterial genomes
BLAST+ (NCBI) or SRST2 is used in conjunction with the Bacterial Antimicrobial Resistance Reference Gene Database (NCBI) Rarity calculated based on hits amongst a species. Currently Rarity scales support E.Coli, Salmonella, Listeria, Vibrio, Staph and Campylobactor. If the organism doesn't correspond to these 6 bacteria, then rarity will be calculated for all bacteria genomes in our database
